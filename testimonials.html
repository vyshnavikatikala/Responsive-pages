ABOUT THE THERAPIST

As a Therapist

           I have created space in a therapeutic setting for people to improve their overall functioning. In my office, it is important for me to create a safe, comfortable, and accepting space without judgment."

I believe that with counselling, people can begin to see their own strengths, and find powerful resources for growth within them to move towards their personal and professional goals, without limitations. I see myself as an enabler in their journey.

Through holistic lens, I view the mind-body-spirit connection as integral part in understanding the client‘s perspective. I draw from different schools of practice, but all of them have a central tenet of placing importance on the clients for allowing them to resonate with their feeling thinking and behavior.
